Pharmacies are generally divided into two main sections: the pharmaceutical section and the counseling section. In the pharmaceutical sector, pharmacies are responsible for the preparation, storage and distribution of medicines. These drugs include prescription drugs, over-the-counter drugs, and health and cosmetic products. The consultation department is another important pillar of pharmacies, which includes specialized consultations in the field of drugs, how to use them, and checking drug interactions. Pharmacists, with their knowledge in this field, help patients to make the best decisions regarding their treatment.
